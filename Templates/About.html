This page will tell you how the password strength tester works and what its good for!

The password strength tester takes any passcode you want to use and give it a score on how secure it is!

This tester is useful for creating secure passwords that are hard to hack and obtain. Keeping a secure password will make sure your personal information is safe on the internet.

A score over 75% is a great password and secure enough to protect your accounts.

A score under 75% is not a secure enough password and would need more special characters or numbers to make it secure.

After your ideal password is deamed secure, copy and paste the password somewhere secure to keep a copy of the passcode.
